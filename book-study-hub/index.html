    How our studies work

    Reading, discussion, and shared learning

    Each book study at Grace Harbor includes a simple structure to help us read together, think deeply, and have meaningful conversations. Everything you need for the current study can be found on its page.

    Weekly Reading Plan

    Each study includes a clear weekly reading plan so everyone knows what to read before the next gathering. Even if you miss a meeting, you can easily follow along and stay connected to the discussion.

    Discussion Prompts

    Each week includes discussion prompts designed to help us think more carefully about what we are reading and how it connects to our lives, our faith, and our understanding of Scripture.

    Study Resources

    Many studies also include helpful resources such as videos, articles, podcasts, or downloadable guides that can deepen your understanding of the book and the ideas it explores.
